Sources & Notes

▾

Scope 2 estimates from EIA MECS 2022 Table 7.6 electricity consumption by sector and Census region, distributed to eGRID 2023 subregions by net generation share. Emission rates converted from lb CO₂/MWh to kg CO₂/MWh.

Full Macrogrid: all subregions equalize to the demand-weighted national average — theoretical maximum.
NIETC Corridors: applies ~40% rate equalization for regions in DOE's 2024 National Interest Electric Transmission Corridor designations (PJM, MISO, SPP-North, WECC, New England, Southeast); Texas and Hawaii excluded.
NERC ITCS: applies ~55% equalization across all connected regions based on NERC's 2023 Interregional Transfer Capability Study findings across 7 studied interfaces; Texas and Hawaii excluded.

All scenarios are static counterfactuals excluding transmission physics and induced renewables buildout. Facility locations from EPA TRI; solar may be undercounted due to TRI thresholds.
